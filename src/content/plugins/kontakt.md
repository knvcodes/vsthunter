Kontakt is the world's leading software sampler, powering thousands of professional library instruments from orchestral scores to experimental sound design tools. Its deep scripting engine allows developers to build highly expressive and playable instruments with custom interfaces. For composers and producers, Kontakt remains an essential platform for accessing high-quality sampled sounds across every genre.
